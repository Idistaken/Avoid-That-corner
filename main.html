Avoid That Corner

RULE 1: The first player places the token any place they like in active section of board . Then, begnning with the Second player and taking turns, each player must move the token from it's current position.

RULE 2: To move the token, a player picks one direction - WEST, SOUTHWEST, SOUTH and moves the token in that direction as many places they'd like.

RULE 3: The player who moves the token into the Bottom-Left RED grid loses the Game
